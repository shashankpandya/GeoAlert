Earth Pulse User Guide

🌍 Earth Pulse v4.0

Earth Pulse is a planetary intelligence platform combining real NASA EONET data with an AI simulation engine to provide the most comprehensive natural disaster monitoring experience available in a browser.

Quick Start: The 3D Globe view is your command center. Rotate by dragging, zoom with scroll, click any marker for event details. The AI panel (brain icon top-right) provides live predictions and insights.
3D Globe: Three.js globe with 500+ pre-seeded events, atmospheric shader, and event markers with severity rings.
AI Engine: Pattern recognition, predictive alerts, compound risk analysis, and natural language summaries.
Alerts: Add up to 5 locations. Get personalized proximity, severity, and type-based alerts with action guides.
Emergency Mode: Press Ctrl+E for full crisis dashboard with survival tools and first aid.

🌐 3D Globe Controls

🖱️ Drag — Rotate the globe

🖱️ Scroll — Zoom in/out

🖱️ Click marker — View event popup

🔄 Auto-rotate — Toggle with the rotate button (top-right)

📍 Markers — Color-coded by severity: Red=Critical, Orange=High, Yellow=Moderate, Cyan=Info

💫 Pulsing rings — Critical and high-severity events pulse to draw attention

🌙 Day/Night — Toggle the terminator line showing Earth's shadow

🤖 AI Intelligence Engine

The AI engine runs continuously on all event data, generating:

  • Pattern Recognition — Detects clusters of events indicating larger upcoming events
  • Predictive Alerts — Estimates probability and timing of follow-on events
  • Cross-Correlation — Links drought patterns to historical famine risks
  • Compound Risk — Identifies regions with simultaneous multiple hazards
  • Natural Language — Plain English summaries updated every 30 seconds
The AI panel is always accessible via the button in the top-right toolbar.

🔔 Alert System

Go to Alerts view to configure personalized alerts.

  • Add up to 5 saved locations (home, work, family)
  • Set proximity radius per location
  • Filter by severity (Critical/High/All)
  • Enable browser push notifications
  • Alerts include What, Where, When, Severity, Action, and Confidence

🚨 Emergency Response Mode

Press Ctrl+E or click the flashing ⚠️ button to activate Emergency Mode.

Emergency Mode provides:

  • AI-generated 3-sentence crisis briefing
  • Offline survival checklist
  • First aid quick reference (WHO standards)
  • Emergency contacts (editable)
  • Risk communication templates
